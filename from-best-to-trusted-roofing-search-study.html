What This Page Connects

The goal is not to say “best roofer near me” louder. The goal is to make trust easier to verify.

This landing page is the public launch hub for the From Best to Trusted research release. It points homeowners, contractors, journalists, search engines, and AI answer systems to the same durable sources: DOI, paper, book, dataset, code/source repository, and companion AI visibility study.
